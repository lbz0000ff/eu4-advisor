TheQingdynasty (1644-1911), also called theManchudynasty, was the last imperial dynasty of China. In 1644, the Chinese capital at Beijing was captured by rebels, upon which the lastMingemperor committed suicide. The Ming dynasty officials and generals called on the Manchus for aid. The Manchus took advantage of the opportunity to seize the capital and establish their own dynasty in China. By adopting the Ming form of government and continuing to employ Ming officials (the in-gameCelestial Empiregovernment), the Manchus managed to rule China until the early 20th century.

Qing does not exist at the beginning of the grand campaign, but can be formed by theManchu, which in turn can be formed byJianzhou,Haixi,Xibe,Orochoni,Nanaior any other country with Jurchen primary culture.
