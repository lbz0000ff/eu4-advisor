Historically, theRoman Empirewas the dominant great power in Europe in the first few centuries AD and the first major empire of its kind and left behind a legacy that would form the very foundation for European civilisation of subsequent ages. While the Western Roman Empire ceased to exist long before the era covered byEuropa Universalis IV, the Eastern Roman Empire continued to survive asByzantium. Any Christian or Pagan nation other than theHoly Roman Empireor thePapal Statescan restore the Roman Empire by decision after re-conquering most of the historical Empire. The other way to play the Roman Empire is to restore the Roman Empire asByzantiuminCrusader Kings IIand converting the save to EU4 using theEuropa Universalis 4 Save Converter.

The Roman Empire can reform into theHoly Roman Empireby becoming emperor of the HRE and passing theRenovatio Imperiireform.

Forming the Roman Empire will convert all provinces of the nation's primary culture group to Roman Culture and set that as the primary culture. This is unique because this is the only way to rediscover alost cultureaside from custom nations, fantasyrandom new worldnations, and console commands.

If the Roman Empire gains a parliament through any reform that grants one (such as Parliamentarism for example), it will be named the Senate as a reference to the Roman Senate which existed throughout the Republican and Imperial periods of Rome.
