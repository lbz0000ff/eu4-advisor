### Missions[edit|edit source]

Spain's missions focus on completing the Reconquista, projecting power into Italy and the Holy Roman Empire, and expanding into theNew Worldand the East Indies.

When forming Spain asAragonor having ever beenAragon, they keep their top half of theirAragonese missionstree, while getting access to the lower half of the Castilian missions, whenDomination DLC is active.

### Events[edit|edit source]

#### Italian Ambition[edit|edit source]

If both Spain andFranceown provinces inItalybetween 1490 and 1590, they can claim each other's lands, reflecting the Italian Wars that consumed the attention of these two rivals in this period. This event type will lead to worsened relations between the nations and a greater likelihood of war.

#### Minor events[edit|edit source]

The Spanish State Bankruptcyevents have returned, but only as flavour events if Spain actually goes bankrupt.
