Trade nodesare 80 static "locations" on themapwheretradecan be conducted utilizing merchants. The province containing a trade node must be discovered before merchants can be sent there, but trade nodes are not otherwise tied to specific provinces. Instead, nodes are tied to a collective of provinces, each contributing its trade value to determine the overall local wealth generated for the node. The node value can be modified by improving base production wealth, trade goodsprice changes, constructingmanufactoriesor steering trade to and from the node.

As a general rule, controlling more provinces in a node helps in gaining hightrade powerwithin that node, although this could also be achieved by controlling only provinces with trade modifiers (i.e.estuary orcenter of trade). Provinces which are added to states or trade companies are more useful for trade power due to their low autonomy floor. Other means of increasing trade power involve sendingmerchants or a fleet oflight ships to the node. Picking certainidea groupsor opportunities to increasemercantilism will improve the effectiveness of taking land for its trade power.
