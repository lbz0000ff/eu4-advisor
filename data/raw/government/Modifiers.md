### Static modifiers[edit|edit source]

Static modifiers are fixed modifiers which define the effects of many game mechanisms: effects ofstability,absolutism,terrain, etc. They can be found in/Europa Universalis IV/Europa Universalis IV/common/static_modifiers/.

### Opinion modifiers[edit|edit source]

Opinion modifiers include being arivalor releasing avassal.

### Triggered modifiers[edit|edit source]

Triggered modifiers are modifiers which can affect any nation fulfilling the requirements. They can be found in/Europa Universalis IV/Europa Universalis IV/common/triggered_modifiers/.

Province triggered modifiers are modifiers which can only affect the nation owning the province where they are based and fulfilling the requirements. They can be found in/Europa Universalis IV/Europa Universalis IV/common/province_triggered_modifiers/.

### Country and province modifiers[edit|edit source]

Country and province modifiers are modifiers added throughevents,missionsordecisions(respectively with thecommandsadd_country_modifierandadd_province_modifier). Once added, they are automatically active until they are either explicitly removed, expired or the province owner changes. They can be found in/Europa Universalis IV/Europa Universalis IV/common/event_modifiers/.

Only province modifiers added with the commandadd_permanbent_province_modifierremain when the province owner changes.

#### Permanent modifiers[edit|edit source]

For a modifier to be permanent (i.e. to apply until the end of the game), it must have a duration of -1.
