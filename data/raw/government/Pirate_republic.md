Apirate republicis a form ofrepublicwhere power is held by a loose collection of pirate crews.

Instead of estates, pirate republics have access to uniquefactionsrepresenting the shifting balance of power dictating their government: theBuccaneers, theSmugglers and theCaptains. They can reenable their estates throughunique government reforms.

### Pirate Election[edit|edit source]

Pirate electionswork mostly the same as the elections of normal republics, besides the fact that electing a candidate will increase the influence of that candidates faction by 20. However they have one important additional feature: legendary pirates. A ruler with theLegendary Pirate trait can be reelected at no cost torepublican tradition. Additionally, depending on the location of the republic's capital region and the date of the game, special historical legendary pirates will be available in the election event. Electing a historical pirate for the first time will also usually gift the nation aheavy ship, named after that pirate's flagship (this ship will be a flagship if the nation does not already have one).

### Coastal raiding[edit|edit source]

Main article:Naval_warfare#Coastal_Raiding

Pirate republics get an extra source of income by raiding coasts of other non-allied nations (or nations with relations over 100) forducats andsailors. Use 3-4 light ships or 4-5 galleys.

### War Against the World[edit|edit source]

Pirate republic can enact tier 6 reform War Against the World. This gives the country a casus belli against all other countries and an ability to demand provinces at0diplomatic cost.

It also disallowsslaves. When a country passes this reform, all its provinces producingslaves are immediately set to produce“unknown”. This will also remove anyTrade Stationsin the province if present, as well as the province modifier“Slave Entrepot”. A new trade good will be randomly reassigned at the beginning of the next month based on the new weights for that province.

### Strategies[edit|edit source]

#### General[edit|edit source]

Pirate republics is a very powerful government type that receives huge income during early game from coastal raiding, and an access to early imperialism-likecasus belli. Being in Asia provides an extra benefit of being able to cause early collapse ofMingthroughdevastationcaused by coastal raiding. SeeEmperor_of_China#Mandate

However, it's impossible to get good outcome duringCourt and Countrydisaster, arguably making it optimal to switch away from this government type duringAge of Absolutism.
