Factions represent powerful and influential groups within the country. Each faction has an influence rating associated with it, and the faction with the highest influence will be in power, granting a set of bonuses (and penalties) to the country. Faction details and interactions can be accessed via the unique faction icon in the lower right corner of the map, next to the HRE and papacy icons.

The influence of a faction can either be increased by spending10monarch powerfrom the faction's category, or will automatically change due to certain modifiers. As the total influence of all factions is always equal to 100, a increase in one faction's influence is at the expense of the two others'.

Revolutionary Republics,Revolutionary States,Revolutionary EmpiresandPirate republicshave theirestatesreplaced by their factions.
