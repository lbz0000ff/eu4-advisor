The Rajputs are an ancient caste of honorable warriors and proud nobles. While the rugged land of Rajasthan is the heart of their power, Rajput nobility can be found throughout northern India and many great Rajas claim Rajput descent.

### Availability[edit|edit source]

General availability:

- Available to all countries in theIndian technology group if theprimary culture is Rajasthani/ Malvi or part of the Hindustani, Central Indian or Eastern Aryan group (except Sinhalese). (see Map).
Specific conditions:

- Does not useother mechanics
- DoesnothaveCelestial Empiregovernment reform
- Isnotnomad
- TheMughalsmay replace their Rajputs estate with the Marathas estate via the "The Maratha Caste" event, triggered by the "Control the Desh" mission
For nations that arenotMuslim,Sikh,Theravada,VajrayanaorMahayana, this estate replaces theNobility Estate. (this mainly concernsHindu nations)
