Due to performance reasons, the list of provinces were split in three: economic, geographical, and political. These each show information for every single province in the game.

Each of the three lists every province ID and name. What they list beyond that is indicated below:

- Economic list of provinces– Development, trade good, trade node, and permanent modifiers
- Geographical list of provinces– Type, continent, sub-continent, region, and area
- Political list of provinces– Owner, culture, culture group, and religion
- Provinces by Region –African regions,Asian regions,East Indian regions,European regions,Indian regions,Oceanian regions,North American regions,South American regions
