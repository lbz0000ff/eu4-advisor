East Indian regionsareregionsthat are part of the East Indiessubcontinent, which belongs toAsia. The regions ofIndiaandother parts of Asiahave their own pages. The table content is out of the files in/Europa Universalis IV/history/provincesand refers to a game start on the 11th of November,1444. Province names listed are the ones they have with the "Dynamic Province Names" option off; names found in game may be different.

Note:Continents, regions and areas of Europa Universalis 4 do not always match reality.
