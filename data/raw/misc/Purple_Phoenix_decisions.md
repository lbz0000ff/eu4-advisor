### Decisions[edit|edit source]

#### Triumph for Greece

With the reconquest of Greece complete we can honor the victorious soldiers and generals with a triumphal march through the capital, a tradition that dates back to the earliest days of Rome. While defunct for many years, our phoenix-like rebirth seems like an apt time to restart it.

ThePurple PhoenixDLC is enabled.The country:

- isByzantium.
- own at least 1 province ofMoreaorNorthern Greeceareas.
- hasnotenacted this decision before.
If the country is AI-controlled then it:

- cannotbe acustom nation.
Byzantiumowns all provinces of Morea and Northern Greece areas.

Byzantium:

- gains10prestige.
- gains25army tradition.
